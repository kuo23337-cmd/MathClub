Why Gamma?

Beauty of Math

We believe math is more than exams. It's a rigorous, beautiful art form. We dive deep into algebra, geometry, and logic to appreciate its true elegance.

Cross-Border Network

Based in Rome, connected to Beijing, and expanding globally. We foster cross-cultural academic exchange among international and domestic high school students.

Breaking Bias

We actively advocate for gender equality in STEM. With strong female leadership, we encourage and empower all minds to conquer complex mathematical challenges.
