X. About the Club

X. 关于数学社

Gamma Math Club originated from a shared conviction that mathematical exploration thrives outside standard testing parameters. We form intellectual connections between international divisions in Beijing and European secondary schools, establishing a global communication channel.

伽马数学社源于一个共同的信念，即数学探索不仅存在于标准化测试之中。我们在北京的国际教育资源与欧洲的中学之间搭建起学术桥梁，建立起国际化的互通合作平台。

Our Core Philosophies

核心发展理念

Transnational Exchange

跨国界交流

Utilizing mathematics as an objective, shared language to communicate analytical ideas between peers from differing cultures.

将数学作为一种客观中立的通用语言，促进来自不同文化背景同伴之间的思想沟通。

Breaking STEM Stereotypes

打破理工科刻板印象

Actively encouraging female leadership and enrollment. Our founding executive structure and continuous seminar program maintain active gender balance.

积极鼓励女性领导力的展现与加入。我们的创始管理团队和研讨会项目致力于维护平衡的性别参与度。

Clear Pedagogy

教学相长

Developing students' presentation skills. Teaching mathematics to others is the most reliable way to formalize and master one's own understanding.

培养学生的演示技能。向他人阐述数学原理是深化并巩固自身认值的极佳方式。

Y. Academic Activities

Y. 学术活动

We balance structured seminars with self-directed community problem challenges. Our scope is strictly high-school bounded (AP/IB/A-Level and domestic equivalents) to ensure accessibility.

我们平衡了体系化的学术研讨与自主驱动的社区挑战。学术范围限定在高中知识领域（AP/IB/A-Level 及同等水平）以确保参与度。

Z. CAS Alignment

Z. CAS 契合度

As international curriculum candidates, our operations satisfy the guidelines of the CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service) project framework, creating demonstrable peer educational value.

作为国际课程项目候选人，我们的运营活动完全契合 CAS (Creativity 创意、Activity 运动、Service 服务) 项目大纲指标，创造可量化的同伴互助教育价值。

Creativity

创意实践

Designing bilingual mathematical slides, scripting educational explanations, and managing a clean, modern digital community architecture.

设计双语数学幻灯片、编写学术原理解析，并维护整洁易用的数字化社群服务架构。

Activity

活动组织

Coordinating real-time academic operations across European and Asian time zones, organizing digital presentations, and managing structural assets.

跨越欧洲与亚洲时区协调、组织实时线上研讨活动，主持数字化发表会议并管理社团资产。

Service

社区服务

Delivering accessible tutoring and peer-review processes, lowering educational barriers, and fostering an inclusive platform for female mathematicians.

提供无障碍的同伴辅导与评审，降低学术交流壁垒，并为女性数学爱好者提供包容平等的平台。

"What is Mathematics?"
