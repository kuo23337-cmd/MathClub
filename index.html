X. About the Club

X. 关于数学社

Gamma Math Club originated from a shared conviction that mathematical exploration thrives outside standard testing parameters. We form intellectual connections between international divisions in Beijing and European secondary schools.

伽马数学社源于一个共同的信念，即数学探索不仅存在于标准化测试之中。我们在北京的国际教育资源与欧洲的中学之间搭建起学术桥梁，建立起国际化的互通合作平台。
